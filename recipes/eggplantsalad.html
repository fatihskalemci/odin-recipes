Roasted Eggplant Salad

Description

This is hardly a salad, it is rather a kind of yoghurt but it just fits with meat and chicken dishes

Ingredients

  • Strained yoghurt
  • Roasted Eggplant (you can roast it yourself)
  • Garlic (grated)
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Mix grated garlic and yoghurt in a bowl
  2. Add roasted eggplant and mix
  3. Spread some olive oil (and some walnuts if you like)
  4. Serve as you please
